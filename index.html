ENHANCED BRAIN IMAGING

Brainscores harnesses the power of AI and multimodal imaging to improve lesion detection, leading to more precise evaluations of complex neurological conditions.

WHERE WE'RE AT

Our prototype is currently being developed, hand in hand with clinical and AI experts. We are confident to launch the useability trials in summer 2024. In the next 12 months, we will validate the platform with our partners.

WHAT'S UNDER THE HOOD

Our software provides an easy to use interface that leverages the following features:

  • Compatible with clinical MRI images and most common protocols
  • Multi-Stream: Multimodal data processing (T1-weighted, FLAIR, Diffusion, Microstructure, Functional)
  • Brain Scorer: Probability based image diagnostic
  • AI-assist: AI technology to highlight significant deviations
  • Case Reporter: Automated radiological report generator
  • Neuro Navigation: Support to download and use our results with neurosurgery assisted hardware
